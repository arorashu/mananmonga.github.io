Zcash Service Status Library

  • ZCash Service Status Dashboard (https://grants.zfnd.org/proposals/761374418-zcash-service-status-dashboard) was built to free up mindspace of Electric Coin Company and ZCash developers and allow them to focus on the core protocol and the ecosystem around it by abstracting the cumbersome task of maintaining awareness around the truth value of various external services that exist in the ZCash ecosystem.
  • Currently building a library for the Zcash ecosystem with the lead engineer of the above grants-winner that will have the potential to replace the health-check dashboard in some ways
